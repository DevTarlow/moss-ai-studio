## Handmade Checker - Live on the Chrome Web Store

**The problem:** Etsy is full of listings that look handmade but were generated by AI. Product photos with artificial lighting, descriptions that read like they were written by a model that's never touched the material it's describing. As a shopper, it's getting harder to tell the difference.

And sometimes that means getting tricked into buying items that don't even exist!

**What it does:** Handmade Checker adds a "Check Listing" button to any Etsy product page. One click and it analyzes the title, description, and main product image to give you a clear verdict: Likely Handmade, Possibly AI-Assisted, or Highly Suspect. It shows you specific reasons behind every score so you can make your own call.

<img src="/images/products/handmade-checker-chrome-extension.png" alt="Handmade Checker" style="width: 50%; display: block; margin: 2rem auto" />

**The model:** Free tier gets you 10 checks per day - enough for casual shopping. Pro is $5/month (or $48/year) and gives you 100 checks per day, detailed evidence breakdowns, a full check history, and auto-scan that checks every listing you visit.

**Who it's for:** Anyone who shops on Etsy and wants to support real makers instead of AI-operated storefronts. Also useful for Etsy sellers who want to check their competition.

**Get it here:** [handmadechecker.com](https://www.handmadechecker.com) - installs directly from the Chrome Web Store.

## MossCrawl - Desktop App for SEO & Niche Research (Coming Early August)

**The problem:** SEO research tools are either expensive SaaS products with monthly subscriptions or they're clunky desktop apps from 2012. If you're doing niche research, keyword analysis, or content filtering, most tools make you jump through hoops to get the data you actually want.

**What it does:** MossCrawl is a desktop app that crawls web pages and scores them against your target keywords. Point it at a set of pages, drop in the keywords, and it tells you exactly how well each page aligns. Full local processing, so your data never leaves your machine. Also, export reports!
