I've been building and selling software since 2014. Thousands of customers, a lot of products, and one big lesson: the best software doesn't come from a room full of people who've never met a user. It comes from someone who actually uses what they build and answers the emails when something breaks.

## What We Ship

Moss AI Studio is the latest version of that idea. A solo-operated studio building AI-powered tools, automation software, and custom applications for people who need things to actually work.

Two products are live right now, and I'm actively developing more.

**Handmade Checker** - A Chrome extension that tells you whether an Etsy listing was actually handmade or generated by AI. One click, a clear score. Free tier, Pro at $5/month. It's already published on the Chrome Web Store and it's been very useful.

**MossCrawl** - A desktop app for SEO research and niche discovery. Drop in keywords, point it at pages, and it scores how well each page aligns with your keywords. Full local processing - your data never leaves your machine. $39 one-time license, coming early August.

And if neither of those fits what you need, I also build custom software - scrapers, automations, browser extensions, AI integrations.

You describe the problem, I quote it, I build it.

## The Philosophy

I don't have a marketing team. I don't have account managers. There's no chatbot between you and the person who wrote made the product. When you buy something from Moss AI Studio or hire me for a custom build, you deal with me from start to finish.

That means:

- You get direct email support from the guy who built it
- If something breaks, I fix it - usually within hours
- I don't overpromise features that don't exist yet
- The pricing is straightforward. No "talk to sales." No hidden fees.

This isn't a side project or a lifestyle business. It's a full-time commitment to building solutions that solve real problems.

## Why Now

AI tools have hit a point where they're actually useful for building real products. Not just demos and wrappers, but tools that do a specific job well and save people time or money. I've been watching this space for years, and the window between "the tech is ready" and "the market is flooded" is exactly where I like to build.

So I'm opening the doors. You can browse the products, grab a license, or reach out about a custom project. Everything on this site is built, tested, and supported by me.
